Dumping Process Memory and Searching for strings with LLDB

It seems that searching for strings live during a debug session should be pretty straight forward. However, I could figure out how to do this with lldb and mem find. Luckily I found another way that unblocked me for now.

lldb attach processname
process save-core /tmp/memory.dump
strings - /tmp/memory.dmp
